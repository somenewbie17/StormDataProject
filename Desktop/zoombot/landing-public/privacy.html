Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 23, 2025

At CourseHub, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Class schedule and course information
  • Institution/university details
  • Login credentials (securely hashed)

Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Usage data and analytics
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

Content and Recordings

  • Class recordings and educational content
  • Transcripts and study materials
  • Notes and files you upload
  • Search queries and platform interactions

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the CourseHub service
  • Record and organize your class sessions
  • Generate transcripts and study materials
  • Send you notifications and service updates
  • Improve our service and develop new features
  • Respond to your requests and support needs
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

3. Content Availability Disclaimer

IMPORTANT: CourseHub operates on a "best effort" basis. We strive to provide access to all scheduled classes, however:

  • No Guarantee of Availability: Technical issues, institutional policies, or operational constraints may prevent content from being available
  • Content Limitations: We are not liable for missing, incomplete, or unavailable recordings
  • Quality Variations: Content quality may vary due to source quality, connectivity, or technical constraints
  • Access Restrictions: Some content may be restricted or unavailable due to institutional or instructor preferences
  • Platform Limitations: Technical or operational changes may affect content availability
  • Storage Limitations: Content may be subject to storage limits and retention periods

You acknowledge that CourseHub is a supplementary tool and should not be your sole method of capturing class content. We recommend taking your own notes and recordings when possible.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

We May Share Information With:

  • Service Providers: Cloud hosting, storage, and analytics providers who help us operate the service
  • Educational Institutions: With your consent, we may share data with your institution for academic purposes
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Limited employee access to personal data

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Cookie Control: Manage cookie preferences in your browser

7. Data Retention

  • Account information is retained while your account is active
  • Recordings may be retained based on your subscription plan
  • Deleted content is removed within 30 days (subject to backups)
  • Some data may be retained for legal or operational purposes

8. Educational Records and Compliance

CourseHub operates in Guyana and primarily serves students at the University of Guyana and other Guyanese educational institutions. We comply with applicable Guyanese data protection and privacy laws. Class recordings and academic content are treated as educational records and protected accordingly.

Multi-Jurisdictional Considerations

While we operate in Guyana, data may be stored using cloud services that operate internationally. Your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in jurisdictions including but not limited to the United States, where data protection laws may differ from Guyanese regulations.

10. Children's Privacy

CourseHub is intended for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or through the service. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
